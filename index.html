The Game

In this game it requaires to find the lost treasure to be a winner. There is only one treasure out of the eight square boxes. Finding the right one will declair the winner.

Players are granted three precious attempts to select the box harboring the hidden gem, and the tension builds as they make their choices. Only the one who correctly identifies the treasure's hiding place will be declared the triumphant winner, their prowess as a treasure hunter celebrated by all who dare to embark on this challenging and unforgettable journey.
